A **lifecycle management system** built for **Siemens** as a digital transformation of “Excel-first” tracking: one place for **installed base**, **repairs**, **projects** (SBB, OBB, Bern, etc.), and device health — so teams see the same truth across engineering and operations.

The product pairs a **Siemens-branded** web app (login, workspaces, home dashboards) with workflows for devices nearing EOL and repair pipelines.
